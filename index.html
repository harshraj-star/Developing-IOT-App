ABOUT PROJECT

Internship Assessment Presentation on IOT
I have completed my training of Developing an IOT Application at CISCO ThingQbator . The name “thingQbator” is a combination of ‘Internet of Things’ and ‘Incubator’.  ThingQbator was started as an internal incubator of IoT ideas coming from Cisco’s engineer community where students can learn about digital technologies in a hands-on environment, turn their ideas into working prototypes. It was a 4 week Training Program and after the completion of training and project they take a test to give certificates
So in this presentation i will focus on my project work where i will discuss about IOT applications using Node MCU ESP8266. Over the past few years, the ESP8266 has been a growing star among IoT or Wifi-related projects.

Objectives

We will see how ESP8266 will host a web page with real time charts that have new readings added every 30 seconds. We will access the data on cloud and visualize the outputs , will use internet communication protocols like REST  to retrieve data from cloud and develop web application. We will use AJAX , JSON , HTML and JavaScript
